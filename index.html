About the Midwest Queer Trans Zine Conference + Fest (mqtzf)

The 2024 Midwest Queer & Trans Zine Fest will be held Saturday, October 19th and Sunday, October 20th, 2024 from 11am-5pm each day at Open Book in downtown Minneapolis, MN.

This year, we are also hosting a one-day Queer & Trans Zine Conference on Friday October 18th at the Elmer L. Andersen Library on the West Bank of the UMN campus.

The Midwest Queer Trans Zine Conference is free and open to the public. Click here to register for the conference.

Follow us on Instagram for future updates.
